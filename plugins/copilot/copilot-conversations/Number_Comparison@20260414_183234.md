9.11 is bigger than 9.8 because when comparing decimal numbers, we look at the digits from left to right:

- Both numbers have the same whole number part (9)
- Comparing the tenths place: 1 (from 9.11) vs 8 (from 9.8)
- Since 1 > 8, 9.11 is greater than 9.8

This might seem counterintuitive because 11 is greater than 8, but in decimal numbers, we're comparing the tenths place (first digit after the decimal), not the hundredths place.
